Who We Are

Lingo has created a way for our users to enhance their language skills while reading the news. As a member of Lingo you will be able to read partially translated articles in either Spanish or French. The best part about Lingo is that it puts learning a new language in real world context so that our users can ultimately start reading in a second language. The more you Lingo, the more you learn!



How to get started

Using Lingo is easy! Simply log-in or register for an account, then choose your preferences. On the preferences page, you'll be asked to choose Spanish or French and one or all of a variety of news topics.

What to expect

Lingo will show you partially translated articles based on your preferences. Get stuck on a word? Just hover over the word and we'll show you the English translation.

Learn with Lingo

At Lingo, we realize that everyone learns at their own pace. If articles are getting too easy, simply update your preferences to the next language level!
